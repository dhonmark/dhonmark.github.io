About Me

I'm Dhon Mark, a creative and passionate self-taught designer residing in the Philippines. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from STI College in General Santos City, Philippines.

I have over a year of experience in graphic design, and I'm seeking an exciting opportunity in a progressive company. My goal is to produce exceptional work that exceeds expectations and aligns with the company's objectives.
